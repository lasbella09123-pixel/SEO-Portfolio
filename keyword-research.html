Keyword Research Case Study

Project Name: torontosteamnclean.ca

Project Overview

The objective of this project was to develop a strategic keyword research plan for Toronto Steam N Clean.ca. The focus was on identifying relevant search terms that aligned with customer intent, supported the business services, and created opportunities for sustainable organic growth.

Challenge

The website required a structured keyword strategy to ensure that every service page targeted relevant search queries. Existing content lacked clear keyword mapping, making it difficult to establish topical relevance and support future SEO efforts.

Approach

  • Conducted industry and competitor keyword research.
  • Analyzed user search intent for cleaning-related services.
  • Identified primary, secondary, and long-tail keyword opportunities.
  • Categorized keywords according to service pages and supporting content.
  • Created a keyword mapping document to avoid keyword cannibalization.
  • Prioritized keywords based on business relevance and content opportunities.

Outcome

The project resulted in a well-structured keyword strategy that provided clear direction for website content, service page optimization, and future content planning. The keyword mapping also established a strong foundation for on-page SEO and long-term organic visibility.

Key Learnings

  • Effective keyword research begins with understanding user intent rather than search volume alone.
  • Proper keyword mapping helps maintain topical relevance across the website.
  • Long-tail keywords provide valuable opportunities for targeting highly relevant searches.
  • A documented keyword strategy improves consistency in future SEO campaigns.
